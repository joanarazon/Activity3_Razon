About Kapampangan Gourmet Catering


Tradition since 1899

The Kapampangan Gourmet Catering was founded in Floridablanca, Pampanga by Donya Joana Eve Razon in 2000. Donya Joana specializes in Kapampangan cuisine and thought of a way to showcase the delicious dishes she makes in every generation.Seasonal Ingredients:

For main dishes, she uses native chicken, pig meat, beef, freshly-picked veggies and exotic ingredients like frog meat, kamaro, bubwit (tiny mice) and hito which are traditionally being eaten in Pampanga.

Best Sellers


Sisig

This Filipino favorite dish originated from Pampanga and is made from different pig parts sauteéd with onions, soy sauce, lime juice. (No mayonnaise in the dish, please!) Usually served on a hot plate, sisig is either the perfect pulutan or the best cure for hangover. = Php 35.00 per serving


Bringhe

Dubbed as the Filipino paella, bringhe is usually served in fiestas and on special occasions. = Php 35.50 per serving


Pindang

Pindang is a sweet and sour dish made from medium-thick slices of beef or pork, where meat strips are marinated and fermented for a few days. = Php 40.00 per serving


Bulang-lang Kapampangan

Bulang-lang Kampampangan is a staple dish that can be served on a hot summer day or during the rainy season. This dish is close to sinigang but instead of using tamarind, Kapampangans use guava fruit to give its broth the distinct sour taste we all love. = Php 50.00 per serving


Betute Tugak

These frogs are stuffed with ground pork and other condiments, then deep-fried to make this exotic, yet very palatable dish. If you’ve never tried fried frogs before, many will claim that they almost like chicken. = Php 30.00 per serving
